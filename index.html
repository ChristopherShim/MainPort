About Me

Hello, my name is Chris! I enjoy creating and designing Websites that are nice to look at. My interest for this comes from being a 90's kid and having grown up alongside the birth of the internet.
‍
Having seen the evolution of website designs from super simple to completely complex, it motivates me to create and contribute.

My own personal goal is to create 2 visual libraries; one for unique component sets that users can easily integrate into their own site and the other serving more as a inspiration piece based on design structures I come across and document.

Here is a list of technologies I have worked with:

  • Webflow
  • CSS
  • Relume/Client-First
  • HTML
  • Javascript
  • Greensock
etc...

On my spare time I enjoying looking at art, whether it's at an art gallery somewhere in NYC or inside Virtual Reality. I believe it's important to learn and understand the different ways a structure can be positioned or how certain color combinations can be portrayed to invoke certain feelings.
