About Me

Hello, my name is Chris! I enjoy designing and developing Websites that are enjoyable to look at. My interest for this comes from being a 90's kid and having grown up alongside the birth of the internet.
‍
Having seen the evolution of website designs from super simple to completely complex, it motivates me to create and contribute.

A couple things I keep in mind when designing a website is the design, SEO and Accessibility. I believe that best highest quality websites should be presentable and able to provide information that is accessible to anyone through any device.
